Travel
  • I've been to over 20 countries, 30 US cities, and counting. For better or worse, it's gotten me a reputation as the "travel hacker" among my friends and family, who I've become the unofficial travel agent for booking free flights with miles and points.

Music
  • I've been in and out of bands since as long as I can remember. I'm primarily a drummer, but most recently performed original music in an acoustic duo which donated our EP profits to the Philadelphia charity Cara's Sweethearts - a non-profit for sick children. In recent years, I've been writing, recording, producing, and mixing my own songs on my home-built computer for my bedroom studio.

Food
